About Me

I am a couples therapist, sexologist, and relationship advisor. My goal is to help partners create healthy, conscious, and safe relationships in which both feel valued and heard.

In my work, I integrate reliable sexological knowledge with empathy and a practical, coaching-oriented approach. I believe that a crisis in a relationship is a natural stage that, when properly processed, can lead to a deeper understanding and rebuild intimacy.

At the same time, I educate on social media. My videos on TikTok and Instagram regarding relationships and sexology have gained huge popularity, generating millions of views and helping couples in daily care for their relationship.
